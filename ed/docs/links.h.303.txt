H.323 Version 2.0 Standards Compatibility
 WebPhone lets you speak with Internet telephone users around the world
 regardless of vendor, platform or network. WebPhone supports the H.323
 Version 2.0 Series Standard including the G.723.1 and G.711 audio codecs,
 allowing interoperability between IP-based multimedia applications. Now you
 can send voice and video communications to any H.323-compliant application.
